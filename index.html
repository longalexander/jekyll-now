I believe that internalizing the wisdom of others makes my life more fulfilling.

First, I would like to help my children lead fulfilling lives. One way I can help is by collecting wisdom for them to learn. The short quotes below might provide answers, spark curiosity, or lead them to explore new ideas.

Second, I would also like to help those I share the world with enjoy the wisdom that has helped me. For this reason, this collection is hosted on a publically accessible website.

I hope you enjoy the collection. If you have any questions or additions feel free to contact me at alexanderlong@stanford.edu
